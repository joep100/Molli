Win #3 · Differentiation

Meet the expectation. Then beat it.

The marketplaces trained everyone to expect fast delivery — that part is now table stakes. The edge for a Dublin shop isn't offering it, it's beating it: in a dense city with short distances, you can put the parcel on the doorstep tonight, not next week.

Expected

Fast is the baseline now

Your customers already judge you against Amazon-speed. Meeting that expectation keeps you in the running; missing it quietly loses baskets you never see.

Tonight

Dublin is small — use it

Most of the city is a short hop from your door. That density is your advantage: same-day and tonight delivery are genuinely realistic here in a way they aren't for a national courier.

Local

The shop they can see

A real address around the corner, delivering the same day, beats a warehouse. It's the one thing a marketplace can't copy — and the reason a browse turns into a regular.
