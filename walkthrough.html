Dublin's same-day delivery network
Stakeholder WalkthroughDemo click-path · ~8–10 min

A page-by-page script for the live demo. Run it on the deployed site, not the preview. The arc below is the story the click-path tells — keep the room's attention moving from the promise to how retailers buy.

The promise→Why we can keep it→The clever bit→It working→How retailers buy→Their home base
1
The hook

Home index.html

  • Open on the hero — "Collect today, deliver tomorrow. Guaranteed." Let the ticker rotate through Dublin areas.
  • Call out pricing from €7.99 and the "Built for" strip — name beauty, health & wellness as target verticals.
  • Scroll to the stats and the one-line USP: the only parcel network that collects same-day and delivers next-day.
2
Why it works

Zones zones.html

  • Show the O'Connell Bridge radius map. The story: a tight radius is what makes same-day at this price possible.
  • City centre = cargo bike (bigger loads); greater Dublin = smaller cap. This sets up the fit tool.
3
The differentiator

Will-it-fit fit.html

  • Drag the sliders; flip the City centre / Greater Dublin toggle to show the envelope change live.
  • This is the memorable, unique moment — linger here.
4
The real engine

Booking booking.html

  • Enter a D2 address (city centre), pick a size, run the sizer at max, add an add-on, walk to the confirmation screen.
  • Emphasise it's a working calculator, not a mockup.
5
The money slide

Business bulk.html / business.html

  • Land on the retailer pitch: credit packs with auto top-up, self-serve, same-day.
  • This is where the room's attention should end up.
6
Close

Account account.html

  • Quick flash of the post-login dashboard: saved addresses, cards, bookings.
  • "This is what a retailer lives in day-to-day."
