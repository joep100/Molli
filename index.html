The name

Named for Molly Malone — Dublin's original one-woman logistics network, hauling stock through streets broad and narrow while the rest of the city was still in bed. No app, no ETA, no proof of delivery — just a wheelbarrow and a fierce set of lungs. We kept the hustle and the barrow (grand, it's a cargo bike now), added live tracking, and left the cockles and mussels to her. Alive, alive-o.
