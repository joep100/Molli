How the rate is worked out

Every quote is real-time — no flat guesses, no surprises at collection.

01

Read the address

At checkout we take the delivery Eircode and find the zone — city centre or greater Dublin.

02

Check the cart

Weight and size are matched against what a bike or van can carry. Too big? The rate quietly hides.

03

Check the clock

Before 2pm shows same-day; after 2pm shows next-morning by 1pm. Weekends handled automatically.

04

Return the price

The exact rate lands in the customer's shipping list in under a second — the same price you'd pay us.
