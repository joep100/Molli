01 What is Molli

A same-day parcel network built for Dublin retailers.

Overnight carriers pick up today and deliver tomorrow. Molli collects your day's orders on a single scheduled run, sorts them at a central hub, and has them on the customer's doorstep by 6pm the same day. It's the one thing the big carriers can't do — and the thing shoppers now expect.

Molli isn't a start-up trying to build a delivery network — it's a new front end for one that already exists. Behind it is an established Dublin same-day courier with its own drivers, vehicles, hub and live routes. Molli productises capacity we already operate, so the network, the economics and the deliveries are proven on day one — not projected.

02 Who is it for

Shops shipping a daily batch of medium-to-high-value orders.

Molli is built for retailers sending several parcels a day of the kind of goods where speed and a careful doorstep win the repeat customer — small, valuable and time-sensitive.

Core sectors

Beauty & skincare · health & wellness · optical · pharmacy · jewellery · boutique & high-end fashion.

The sweet spot

City-based senders shipping 10+ orders a day — where one rider carries several drops on a single run, so same-day works economically.

In practice

A Dundrum pharmacy sends ~25 orders a day: collected on the daily run, sorted at the hub, delivered by 6pm. We're built to scale that.

Molli has one rule: same-day needs a minimum of 10 orders a day — and it's the model, not a sales gate. Same-day runs on shared runs: one rider collects your batch and clears several drops on a single loop, and that shared loop is what makes a 6pm promise affordable. Below roughly ten a day a run can't be filled — the economics, and the deadline, stop holding. Clear the batch and everything else is flexible: no contracts, no lock-in.

05 Vehicles

The right vehicle for the route.

Inside the canals a bike beats a van door-to-door; over longer distances the motorbike takes over. Matching the vehicle to the route is what keeps us fast and zero-fuss.

City centre · D1–D8

Cargo bike

Zero-emission and unbeatable in traffic — bus lanes, no gridlock, parks at the door. Carries all three sizes, up to 80×60×60 cm and 30 kg.

Greater Dublin · D9–D24

Motorbike

Takes the open road past the city core where distance makes the bike win. Carries up to 40×30×30 cm and 8 kg.

Because the cargo bike carries the big loads, the largest parcels are a city-centre service — within roughly 3 km of the centre, where the bike can reach the door.

07 Cut-off times

Beat the cut-off, it's there by 6pm today.

One run a day, everything delivered by 6pm. Your cut-off depends on where we collect — earlier further out, so the batch still makes the 6pm deadline.

Greater Dublin · D9–D24

Order by 1pm

Miss your cut-off and the order simply drops onto the next working day's run, delivered by 6pm — same flat price either way. The live countdown badge shows shoppers the deadline in real time.
